Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026  ·  Effective: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Collegare Studio ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use Creator Inbox OS — a subscriber-only tool for content creators to manage brand partnership emails and deals. Please read this carefully before using the app.

1. Who We Are

Creator Inbox OS is operated by Collegare Studio. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at hello@collegarestudio.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Access

To access Creator Inbox OS, you provide your email address. We verify it against our subscriber list. We do not collect passwords — access is granted by email verification only.

2.2 Google Account Data (Optional)

If you choose to connect your Google account to access Gmail and Google Calendar features, we request the following Google OAuth scopes:

  • Gmail read access — to display your inbox emails inside the app
  • Google Calendar read/write access — to display and create calendar events
We do not store your Gmail messages or calendar data on any server. All email and calendar data is processed in your browser and stored locally on your device only (via localStorage).

2.3 Data You Create in the App

All data you enter into Creator Inbox OS — including opportunities, brand details, deal notes, templates, team members, and calendar events — is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

2.4 Usage Data

We do not currently use analytics trackers, cookies, or third-party tracking scripts in Creator Inbox OS.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the email address you provide solely to:

  • Verify you are an active subscriber and grant access to the app
  • Communicate important updates about the product (if you have opted in)
  • Respond to support requests you initiate

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

4. How We Store Your Data

Creator Inbox OS is a client-side application. The vast majority of your data — deals, templates, notes, imported emails — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to any server.

Your subscriber email address is retained by us solely to manage access. We do not operate a database of your in-app activity.

Important: Because data is stored locally, clearing your browser storage or switching devices will result in data loss. We recommend using the CSV/PDF export feature regularly to back up your opportunities.

5. Google OAuth & Third-Party Services

When you connect your Google account, you are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We use Google's OAuth 2.0 flow to obtain an access token that is stored locally in your browser. We do not see or store your Google password.

Your access token is used only to make API calls directly from your browser to Google's servers. We do not proxy, intercept, or log these requests.

You can revoke our access to your Google account at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfer: If Collegare Studio is acquired or merges with another entity, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction — you will be notified in advance

7. Data Retention

We retain your subscriber email address for as long as your subscription is active. If you cancel your subscription or request deletion, we will remove your email from our access list within 30 days.

Since all in-app data is stored in your browser, you can delete it at any time by using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings, or by clearing your browser's site data.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@collegarestudio.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Creator Inbox OS is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your subscriber email from unauthorised access. However, no system is completely secure. Since your in-app data lives in your browser, you are responsible for keeping your device and browser secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we will notify active subscribers by email.
